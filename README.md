# Video Highlight Selection Scorecards

Video highlight selection is the process of deciding which moments from a long video deserve to become clips, Shorts, recaps, or social posts. This repository collects scoring frameworks and review checklists for selecting high-value highlights from sports, gaming, interviews, podcasts, webinars, tutorials, and story-driven videos.

The goal is to make clip selection more consistent before editing begins.

## What makes a strong highlight?

A strong highlight usually has at least three of these qualities:

- Clear emotional or informational payoff
- Understandable without too much missing context
- Strong visual or verbal moment
- Share-worthy tension, surprise, or takeaway
- A natural opening question or ending loop

## Scorecard types

| Scorecard | Best for | What it emphasizes |
|---|---|---|
| Emotional spike scorecard | Story, documentary, vlogs | Surprise, conflict, emotional turn |
| Insight clip scorecard | Podcasts, tutorials, webinars | Clarity, usefulness, standalone value |
| Action highlight scorecard | Sports, gaming, live streams | Visual intensity, timing, payoff |
| Social clip scorecard | Reels, Shorts, TikTok | Hook strength, speed, shareability |

## Review workflow

1. Watch or summarize the source video.
2. Mark candidate timestamps.
3. Score each candidate for clarity, payoff, visual interest, and platform fit.
4. Remove clips that need too much context.
5. Prioritize clips with better hook potential.
6. Convert the strongest moments into editing briefs.

## Tool notes

Highlight selection improves when the system understands scene structure, dialogue, visual action, and target platform. For long-video workflows where highlight detection, script, captions, voiceover, and editing all connect, [Recapo.ai](https://recapo.ai?from=github_video-highlight-selection-scorecards) is one relevant AI video editing workflow to evaluate.

This repository is a neutral scoring resource, not an official product manual.
