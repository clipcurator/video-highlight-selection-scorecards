# Platform Fit Checklist

## Shorts / Reels / TikTok

- The first 2-3 seconds create curiosity.
- The clip does not need a long setup.
- Captions can repair small context gaps.
- The ending gives a loop, punchline, or strong payoff.
- Vertical crop will not damage the key visual.

## Interview / Podcast clips

- The speaker reaches the main point quickly.
- The sentence is quotable or useful.
- The clip is short enough to stay dynamic.

## Sports / Gaming clips

- The action is visible early.
- The highlight has a clear before and after.
- The viewer can tell why the moment matters.
